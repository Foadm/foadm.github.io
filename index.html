Kendo Creatives specializes in video storytelling at the intersection of culture, innovation and strategic communication. Understanding human emotions as the center of any unforgettable story, we help brands build authenticity and customer loyalty. Driven to understand your brand, your users and your goals, we will help you build an artistic vehicle to bring it all together.

Shirin Mozaffari is an award winning creative producer and director. She has been producing marketing and educational videos for the past twelve years, working with companies / institutions such as Google and Harvard University.

She has exhibited her short films and animations widely in film festivals in Spain, France, Poland, Sweden, Berlin, Cairo, and New York. Originally from Tehran. Mozaffari received a Bachelor of Arts in Photography from San Francisco State University and a Master of Fine Arts in Film, Video, New Media from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago.
